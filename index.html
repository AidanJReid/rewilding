Rewilding

In rich countries like Ireland, we’re all very aware of the need to do something about climate change. We want to save the rainforests, and reduce our carbon footprint. But we deforested our landscape hundreds of years ago. When we ask Brazilians to stop chopping down trees, we’re standing on very shaky ground! Let’s do something about it. With the growth in vegetarianism, better farming practices and the movement to drive to carbon neutrality, there’s never been a better time to think about rewilding our own country. We’re just getting started, but please let us know if you’d like to help!
